# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Young children (the Malafa kids, Ava ~6 and Kai ~4) exploring the 2026 World Cup
together with a parent. They are pre- or early-readers on a tablet or phone, in
short, playful sessions. The interface is read aloud as often as it is read, so
copy is simple and every action is large, obvious, and forgiving.

## Product Purpose

A delightful learning explorer for World Cup 2026: today's matches, a world map
of competing nations, side-by-side country comparisons, hero stories, and fun
facts. Success is a child who keeps tapping — discovering countries, flags, and
players — and a parent who trusts it is age-appropriate, accurate, and calm.

## Brand Personality

Joyful, worldly, refined. Three words: **celebratory, global, trustworthy.**
It should feel like a beautifully made children's atlas — festive like a
tournament, but composed and grown-up enough that a parent enjoys it too. Warmth
comes from colour, motion, and friendly type, never from clutter.

## The Malafa identity

The family spans four homelands — **Cameroon, Netherlands, Lebanon, and USA** —
and the brand is literally a blend of their four flags. Red is the one colour in
all four, so it leads. The signature device is the **Unity Ribbon**: four bands
(red · gold · green · blue) for the four homes, "Four Homes, One Game."

## Anti-references

- Generic "kids app" defaults: primary-bright rainbow soup, Comic Sans energy,
  literal sky-blue-and-grass-green backdrops, sticker clutter.
- Corporate sports-app coldness (dark navy dashboards, dense stat tables).
- AI-cream / sand / parchment backgrounds. The canvas is a cool near-white.

## Design Principles

1. **Four homes, one game.** The palette and the Unity Ribbon always trace back
   to the four family flags. Colour carries meaning, not decoration.
2. **Refined, not childish.** Jewel tones over primaries; clean white cards over
   sticker chaos. Friendly enough for a 4-year-old, polished enough for a parent.
3. **Big, forgiving, obvious.** Large tap targets, one clear action per moment,
   graceful fallbacks (flags, data, offline).
4. **Calm celebration.** Motion and colour create delight in moments, never a
   page that shouts. Respect reduced motion.
5. **Accurate and inclusive.** Real flags, real greetings, real geography. Every
   text/background pair meets WCAG AA.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. All body and badge text verified ≥4.5:1. Continent colours are
chosen to stay mutually distinguishable (and paired with text labels, never
colour alone). Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Large touch targets for
small hands.
